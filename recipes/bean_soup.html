Soup!

Soups can be anything someone has available to scrounge up, reiterated into a heartwarming meal.

Ingredients

  • Beans of your choice
  • Rice, or any alternative grains
  • A choice of root vegetable
  • Broccoli or Cauliflower
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Creativity

Steps:

  1. Soak 1 1/2 cups of choice of beans overnight, then rinse throughly
  2. Add 2 - 3 litres of water to a large pot, then add beans, along with 1 cup of choice of grains
  3. Bring pot to a boil before adding 1 cup of root vegetable and 1 whole onion
  4. *This is where creativity becomes an ingredient*
  5. Add 1 tablespoon of salt
  6. Add 1 - 2 tablespoons of turmeric
  7. Add pepper to taste
  8. Soup is finished when beans and root vegetables are soft
