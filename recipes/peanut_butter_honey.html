Peanut Butter and Honey Sandwich

The peanut butter and honey sandwich is a staple for those looking for a high protien meal without meat. It's relatively inexpensive and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Bread
  • Honey

Steps:

  1. Evenly spread 2 tablespoons of peanut butter on choice of bread
  2. Incorperate 1 tablespoon of honey onto peanut butter with a gentle mixing technique
  3. Drop second piece of bread onto mixture
  4. Enjoy!
