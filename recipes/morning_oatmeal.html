Morning Oatmeal

The perfect breakfast for everyday, inlcuding a mix of seeds, spices, fruit, and sweetness!

Ingredients

  • Instant Oats
  • Almond Milk (or any alternative)
  • Salt
  • Cinnamon
  • Turmeric
  • Cardamom
  • Blueberries
  • Sunflower Seeds or pumpkin seeds
  • Maple Syrup
  • Chia Seeds

Steps:

  1. Add 1 cup of oats to a bowl, followed by almond milk to cover completely
  2. Mix 1 teaspoon each of cinnamon, turmeric, and cardamom.
  3. Mix 1/2 teaspoon of salt into wet oats and spices
  4. Incorperate one handful of blueberries into mixture
  5. Add 2 tablespoons each of chia seeds and sunflower/pumpkin seeds
  6. Drizzle 1 to 2 tablespoons of maple syrup on top
  7. Place bowl in microwave on high for 2 to 2 1/2 minutes, depending on microwave strength
  8. Mix and enjoy!
